# Chapter 25: The two categorical layers (control-flow and Kleisli)

> **Status:** Stub. Full chapter pending.

**Coming in book v0.2.** The categorical structure of a Clifford program: each automaton as a small category (control-flow), each automaton's mutators as the morphisms of the Kleisli category of the State monad over its fields (data-flow). The two views are complementary. The GA orthogonality check is the existence proof for the *product Kleisli category*, not the product control-flow category. **Bibliography:** §3, §7, §8.
